MONSTER TRUCK

I created this fun experience on monster truck with Unity engine.

For this game I had to make:

  • Utilize speed, engine power, and torque to control the truck in mid-air and pull off stunts and maneuvers.
  • Use dual wheel joints for smoother bouncing and rotate both wheels simultaneously to emulate the maneuverability of a 4x4 vehicle.
  • Wheels friction and ground friction.
  • A Reset-button to recovery.
